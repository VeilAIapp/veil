Veil

Privacy Policy

What we know about you, how we use it, and why the answer to both is almost nothing.

Last updated: May 2025

Veil runs entirely on your device. Your conversations never leave your phone — not to our servers, not to anyone's servers, not anywhere. That's not a policy. That's how the app is built.

01

What We Collect

Almost nothing. Here's the complete list:

Nothing you say to the AI. Your conversations are processed entirely on your device using an on-device AI model. We have no server that receives your messages. We have no database storing your chats. We literally cannot read what you write.

Nothing about your device. We don't collect device identifiers, hardware specs, or usage analytics.

Optional donation data. If you choose to support Veil through Ko-fi, that transaction is handled entirely by Ko-fi. We receive no financial information.

No chat logs No analytics No tracking No ads No accounts
02

How the AI Works

Veil uses an on-device AI model that is downloaded to your phone once and runs locally. When you send a message, it is processed entirely within your device's memory. No network request is made. No data is transmitted.

The AI model itself is a general-purpose language model. It has no knowledge of who you are and no connection to any external service during conversations.

03

Memory Feature

Veil includes an optional memory system that learns facts about you over time — things like your name, preferences, or interests — to personalize future conversations.

All memories are stored exclusively on your device using your phone's local storage. They are never transmitted anywhere. You can view, edit, or delete any memory at any time from within the app. You can clear all memories instantly.

04

Third-Party Services

Veil uses the following third-party services in limited ways:

HuggingFace — The AI model file is downloaded from HuggingFace servers on first launch. After download, no further connection is made. HuggingFace's privacy policy governs that download request.

Ko-fi — The "Support Veil" link opens Ko-fi in your browser. Any donation is governed by Ko-fi's privacy policy. We receive no personal data from this.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or any other third-party tracking.

05

Data Storage & Security

All data Veil stores — your settings, saved chats, and memories — lives in your device's local app storage. It is protected by your device's standard security (PIN, biometrics, encryption). We have no access to it.

If you uninstall Veil, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

06

Children's Privacy

Veil is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will assist with removal.

07

Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Since we collect almost nothing, meaningful changes are unlikely. But if Veil ever adds features that involve data collection, we'll be transparent about it here before shipping.
